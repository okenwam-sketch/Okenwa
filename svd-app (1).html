Content rules
Quality over quantity — every post intentional, planned 4 weeks ahead

Email list strategyOwn it
Your most valuable asset long-term
Collect at every ticket sign-up, merch purchase, event
Welcome sequence: thank you → who SVD is → what's coming
Send on: song drop, event launch, merch drop, ThinkLeafy news
Unlike Instagram, you can't be shadowbanned from your own list
Tool: Mailchimp free tier (up to 500 contacts free)
Press & blogsMonthly
1 outreach per month from Aug onwards
Target: Earmilk, Ones To Watch, Link Up TV, GRM Daily
Local culture mags: Time Out London, Evening Standard music
Pitch angle: SVD artist + events + CBD brand = full movement story
One decent write-up > 50 social posts for credibility
Always send a press kit: bio, photo, links, best track
Sync licensingPassive income
Get paid when your music is placed in content
Register with: Musicbed, Artlist, Epidemic Sound (as creator)
Also: Sync licensing via AWAL, DistroKid sync, Songtrust
TV shows, adverts, YouTube, podcasts all license music
Start after you have 6-10 strong tracks ready
Reaches audiences you'd never find on social media
CollaborationsQuarterly
1 feature per quarter from Aug onwards
Target: similar-level UK RnB artists (2k-20k followers)
Cross-pollinates both audiences instantly
Offer your event platform + your audience as value trade
No label can block your own-crowd collabs
Platform: SVD events = social proof to approach artists
Instagram Live strategyMonthly
1 Live per month — 20 mins max
Piano WIP: "working on something new" — plays into the series
Behind the scenes of a shoot or event setup
Builds intimacy that converts followers to real fans
Announce it 24hrs ahead on Stories to drive viewers
Save it — repurpose as a Reel clip after
Producer deal structureDo now
Protect yourself before the money comes
Agree in writing: who owns the master, who owns the beat
Typical split: 50/50 on beats he makes for you
Your own beats you enhance: 70/30 or 80/20 in your favour
Monthly output agreement: X tracks per month minimum
Use a simple one-page beat licence agreement (free templates online)
ThinkLeafy long gameExit play
Build the brand while weed is still grey area
CBD drink now → CBD vapes next → full wellness brand
When cannabis legalises in UK: company is already established
Register as a Ltd company early — creates proper share structure
Document every sale, every retail partner, every event unit moved
That data = valuation when you want to sell shares or exit
